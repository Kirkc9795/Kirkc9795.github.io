Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook fettuccine in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add Parmesan cheese, stirring constantly until melted and smooth.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine and toss to coat in the sauce.
  7. Garnish with parsley if desired. Serve immediately.

Four Cheese Ravioli

Ingredients

    Pasta dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs

    • Cheese filling

  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup crumbled gorgonzola or fontina cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)

    • Serving

  • Butter or tomato sauce (for topping)
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional garnish)

Instructions

  1. Make the pasta dough by forming a mound of flour with a well in the center. Add eggs and mix until combined. Knead until smooth and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix all four cheeses in a bowl. Add salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste.
  3. Roll out the dough into thin sheets. Place small spoonfuls of cheese filling evenly spaced on one sheet.
  4. Cover with another sheet of dough, press around the filling to seal, and cut into ravioli shapes.
  5. Boil ravioli in salted water for 3–4 minutes or until they float to the top.
  6. Serve with melted butter or your favorite tomato sauce. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.

Creamy Garlic Penne Pasta

Ingredients

  • 12 oz penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook penne pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the flour and cook for 1 minute to form a roux.
  4. Slowly whisk in milk and heavy cream. Bring to a simmer, stirring frequently until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add Parmesan cheese and stir until melted and smooth.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Add cooked penne to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  8. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired. Serve hot.
